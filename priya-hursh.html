Priya & Hursh

Priya and Hursh wanted a contemporary aesthetic for their Indian wedding for a perfect modern and traditional blend. We chose a color palette with pastels, neutrals, and small elements of vibrant shades. We paired the groom with the bride using a shade of gray present in her lehnga embroidery and used the same gray as an accent shade for the bridal party. The parents on both sides were given pastel shades of green, also chosen from the bride’s lehnga.
